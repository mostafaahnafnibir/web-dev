Rajuk Uttara Model College (RUMC) stands as a premier beacon of academic excellence in Dhaka, consistently ranked among the top educational institutions in Bangladesh. Located at Shahjalal Avenue, Sector-6, Uttara, Dhaka-1230, the college is situated just about one kilometer north of Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport. Established in 1994 , RUMC isrenowned for its rigorous academic standards and currently accommodates approximately 8,000 students across its morning and day shifts. Beyond textbooks, the institution fosters a culture of holistic development by encouraging participation in diverse co-curricular activities, ensuring its students are well-prepared for leadership roles in both higher education and professional life.
